CardSnap Golf ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and display name.
  • Golf Data: Scorecard photos, player names, scores, and round history that you input into the app.
  • Device Information: Basic device information for app functionality and crash reporting.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our golf scoring and calculation services
  • Store your round history and scorecard photos
  • Process your subscription payments

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. Scorecard photos are stored in secure cloud storage. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data in transit and at rest.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For secure data storage and authentication

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us or using the delete account feature in the app.

Your Rights
