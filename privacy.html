Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

Introduction

CardSnap Golf ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address and display name when you create an account.
  • Golf Data: Scorecard photos, player names, scores, and round history.
  • Device Information: Basic device information for app functionality and crash reporting.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our golf scoring and calculation services
  • Store your round history and scorecard photos
  • Process your subscription payments
  • Send important updates about the app

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data in transit and at rest.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Secure data storage and authentication
  • AWS Textract: OCR scorecard scanning
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management
  • Apple App Store: Payment processing

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, and export your personal data. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time using the delete account feature in the app.

Children's Privacy

CardSnap Golf is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
